What Is Tapway?

Tapway provides a new last mile delivery solution to power same day delivery for retailers and businesses. Integrate Tapway into your ecommerce site or book a local delivery today. Tapway gives customers more – Tracking, Security, Insurance and well vetted riders

Realtime Tracking

Real time GPS tracking of in-progress orders. Order history and detailed billing all in one location

Security

All trips are insured to cover goods, from pickup to drop offs. From the moment your merchandise is picked up by Tapway, until the time it is delivered to the customer, we’ve got you covered.

Focus on your business. Tapway handles the Rest

On your schedule

Your customers expect to be able to get items when and where they want them. We deliver on that promise. We enable you to offer on-demand deliveries and custom scheduled delivery windows – today, tomorrow, or up to 30 days out.

Packaging

Efficient for the merchants. Mindful for the consumer. With our same day service, less packaging is required. Meal subscriptions can be packed for hours not days of transport. Electronics require less padding. Clothing requires a bag not a box. Items can be up to 50 pounds and up to the size of a 42-inch TV.

Insurance – You’re Covered

Our insurance includes: Up to #100,000 per delivery for theft, damage or loss of merchandise while Tapway is providing delivery services. Commercial general liability insurance. #1,000,000 motor vehicle liability. #10,000,000 umbrella/excess liability policies. State-required auto insurance that drivers must carry in order to provide delivery services for Tapway. Coverage this extensive is one more reason why some of the largest retailers are opting to include the Tapway same day delivery option for their customers.
